### Got questions?

- ### What is the Experience plan 5-Year price guarantee?











New and existing accounts on an Experience postpaid voice plan can rest assured that the price of talk, text and data won’t change for at least 5 years as long as you are on an Experience plan. The guarantee also applies to price for data on wearable/tablet/mobile internet lines added to your plan. Your guarantee starts when you activate or switch to an eligible plan and doesn’t restart if you add a line or change plans after that. Taxes & fees, per-use charges, plan add-ons, third-party services (e.g. streaming services, international data and satellite services) and network management practices aren’t included.

- ### Is satellite service included with my Experience Beyond plan?











Yes, T-Satellite is included on us with Experience Beyond and Go5G Next plans. Customers on other plans can add the service for a limited time at $10/month (a savings of $5/mo.), per line. Learn more at [https://www.t-mobile.com/coverage/satellite-phone-service](https://www.t-mobile.com/coverage/satellite-phone-service).

- ### How does the Experience plan 5-Year price guarantee work?











Your guarantee starts when you activate or switch to an eligible plan and we won’t change the price of talk, text and 5G smartphone data on our network for at least five years, as long as you are on an Experience plan. Adding or changing to a different Experience plan doesn’t restart the 5-year period.

- ### What does it mean for my plan to have Unlimited Premium Data on the Best Mobile Network?











Most unlimited plans give you an amount of data at the start of your bill cycle where you are prioritized higher than customers who are heavy data users. This data is called Premium Data. Once you have used that data, you are prioritized lower than other customers and you may notice lower speeds in times and places with network congestion. All of our new Experience plans include unlimited premium data, including Experience Beyond and Experience More with 55+/First Responder and Military Savings plans. T-Mobile has been awarded the Best Mobile Network in the U.S based on analysis by Ookla of Speedtest Intelligence® data 2H 2025.

- ### What do “upgrade ready,” “yearly upgrade,” mean?











Our Experience plans have benefits that give you the freedom to upgrade when you want. Upgrade as early as every year with Experience Beyond or be upgrade-ready every two years with Experience More. This is unlike AT&T and Verizon, who quietly increased device contracts from two years to three—with no shorter option if you want their best phone deal.

- ### How does the yearly upgrade benefit work on Experience Beyond?











With Experience Beyond, you’ll automatically become eligible for a yearly upgrade.



1. Purchase a new phone with any eligible promotion on T-Mobile’s no-interest phone payment plan (EIP) via 24 monthly bill credits plus tax and keep it active on an Experience Beyond plan for at least six months.

2. Once half of your phone is paid off, you can trade in your device in good working condition. Then T-Mobile will cover the remaining half of your device cost so you're ready to upgrade.

3. Your yearly upgrade benefit allows you to take advantage of any eligible in-market phone promotions. You’re guaranteed the same great phone deals as a new T-Mobile Experience Beyond customer.



      For example, if you purchase a $1,000 smartphone with Experience Beyond, you can trade it in toward a new one once you pay half ($500) and it's past 6 months. T-Mobile covers the remaining $500 on the old phone. New T-Mobile customers and existing customers on Experience Beyond always get the same great deals when they’re ready to upgrade.

**Experience More**

With Experience More, you will automatically become eligible for an upgrade every two years by taking advantage of any eligible in-market phone promotions.

1. Purchase a new phone with any eligible promotion on T-Mobile’s no-interest phone payment plan (EIP) via 24 monthly bill credits plus tax and keep it active on an Experience More plan.

2. After two years, you're ready to upgrade and can take advantage of any eligible in-market phone promotions. New T-Mobile customers and existing customers on Experience More always get the same great phone deals when they’re ready to upgrade.

- ### What does “new and existing customers get the same phone offers” mean?











Existing customers on qualifying plans always get the same great device deals as new customers switching to T-Mobile on those plans. This is a promise, not a limited-time offer.
